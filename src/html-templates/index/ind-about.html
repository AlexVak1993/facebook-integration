About us

BVBA M. Deleare Building Contractors is a long-established, independent building services and home improvements company based in Tavistock, carrying out work all across West Devon.

We have a wealth of experience working as main building contractors on all kinds of projects, big and small, from home maintenance and improvements to extensions, refurbishments and new builds.

We even work with architects and interior designers where needed to ensure your project is perfectly designed and built.
